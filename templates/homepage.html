AI in HealthCare

In healthcare, delays can mean the difference between life and death; so Healthzoid - our all-in-one disease prediction platform can predict and detect early onset diseases with just a click of a button! A significant AI use case in healthcare is the use of ML and other cognitive technologies like deep learning disciplines for medical diagnostic purposes. Using patient data and other information according to the disease, can help doctors and other medical providers deliver more accurate diagnoses and treatment plans. Machine Learning

In the past decade, the application of machine learning (ML) to healthcare has helped drive the automation of physician tasks as well as enhancements in clinical capabilities and access to care. Natural Language Processing

Making sense of human language has been a goal of AI researchers since the 1950s. In healthcare, the dominant applications of NLP involve the creation, understanding and classification of clinical documentation and published research. NLP systems can analyse from unstructured huge amounts of data and make sense of it in a way to make our lives easier.
